7 latest inovations in robotics

Madanapuri Naveen

23 sep

Google's worker robots, Google is planning to produce worker robots with personalities. The technology giant recently won a patent for the ambitious project.Multi-tasking bots, Momentum Machines developed a multi-tasking bot capable of preparing a gourmet hamburger in as little as 10 seconds.UR3 arm,An automated device created by Universal Robots known as UR3 can build its own replacement parts on the fly. Saul Robot, Paro is a therapeutic robot with the appearance of a baby harp seal. The furry device is intended to help lower stress levels and stimulate interaction between caregivers and patients.
